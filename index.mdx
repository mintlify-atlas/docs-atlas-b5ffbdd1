## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Paste or type your code">
    Open CodeAura and paste your snippet into the live editor. CodeAura automatically detects the programming language and applies syntax highlighting.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize the appearance">
    Use the right-hand panels to choose a syntax theme, adjust the background, pick a font, and style the window frame to match your aesthetic.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export or share">
    Click **Export** to download a high-resolution image, copy it to your clipboard, or generate a shareable link that others can view and remix.
  </Step>
</Steps>
